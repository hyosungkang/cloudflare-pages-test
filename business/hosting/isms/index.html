Email to SMS allows you to send SMS (TXT) messages easily with any email program such as Outlook. It's that simple!

  • No software to install
  • Send an SMS (TXT) to any mobile phone from your email
  • Send individual and bulk SMS from your email
  • Receive email SMS replies back to your inbox
  • Receive original message with any replies
  • Reliable, instant delivery
  • NZ & International networks supported

The SMS API allows you to easily connect to our SMS gateway, integrating SMS (TXT) capability into your website or software systems.

  • Developer friendly, easy-to-integrate product
  • Turnkey code examples, SDK available for .NET, Java, PHP
  • Two-way messaging functionality
  • Real time delivery of reply SMS (web push)
  • Delivery tracking
  • Replies matched back to outbound SMS
  • NZ & International networks supported
