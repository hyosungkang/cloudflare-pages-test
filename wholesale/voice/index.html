Voice

Choose from a range of voice solutions to fit your needs, from big to small.

0800 4 PARTNER
Voice Platform Access

Manage your customers’ solutions with complete control and visibility on our robust, scalable voice platform. Wholesaler access enables you to set up each of your customers’ voice accounts, configure a Cloud PABX, set up a SIP trunk, or configure a fax box to enable fax to email and email to fax. With intuitive apps and features, using the platform is simple and enables you to cater for small to enterprise level customers.

Cloud PBX

A feature rich voice solution providing traditional PABX functionality plus more. Set welcome messages & menu's, time of day routing, ring groups and even fax to email & email to fax.

Sip Trunks

Cost Effective and reliable carrier grade SIP trunks. Pick the solution that works for you from a single carrier trunk delivering calling to your own multi-tennanted PABX or use your wholesale portal to setup individual customer SIP Trunks in minutes.

Tollfree

We offer NZ and international tollfree numbers, enabling NZ businesses to be reached no matter where their customers are calling from. We have call routing options to cater for most business needs, and competitive wholesale calling rates.

International Numbers

Connect your customers calling from outside of NZ and expand your presence with our International Regional Numbers. Select from a variety of countries and connect them back to your Voyager hosted service in NZ.

Cloud Softphone

Our softphone app is available for download on mobile devices, or login from your laptop or desktop computer. No matter where your customers are, they can take their office number with them and maintain seamless business continuity.

Number Portability

Your customers can keep their direct dial number range by porting them to us and we will deliver it to your hosted voice platform or your SIP trunk as required.

iSMS

With over 90 percent of messages read in the first 90 seconds, iSMS is the reliable, low-cost way to reach anyone in the world with a mobile phone. Use our web portal, REST API, or Email to SMS to send messages via our fast, reliable gateway.
