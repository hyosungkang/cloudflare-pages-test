IP Transit

We offer international, Domestic and blended transit with a range of billing and traffic profile options to fit your requirements.

Express Route

Connect privately to your favourite cloud content provider such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure over our high-speed data network.

Backhaul Solutions

With our Layer 2 backhaul service, we can shift your data exactly where it needs to go. Whether it’s throughout NZ or across the ditch to Australia we can get your data where it needs to go.

Data Circuits & Broadband

Let our highly available network help your customers connect. Voyager wholesale can work with you to create a solution that fits for your business. Using our national reach and relationships with all major connectivity providers Voyager can offer you and your customers the best service available at your location, such as UFB, xDSL, Business grade Fibre, and Dedicated point to point circuits. Select unlimited usage plans or utilise your own transit policy. It’s up to you.

Trans-Tasman

Leverage your international network off ours. We can provide low latency, resilient Layer 2 or Layer 3 services over our trans-Tasman network.
