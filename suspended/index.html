Oops, we have a problem!

According to our records, it appears that your Voyager broadband internet account is overdue.

We have endeavoured to contact you via email in addition to sending your monthly invoice statements highlighting when payment is due.

If you believe your account is current, please contact the Voyager credit services team using the details below.

Make a payment to reconnect

Your internet access has now been restricted, although you can still access the Voyager website to make a payment to reconnect your services.

Make an online payment by credit card at pay.voyager.nz or by calling us using the details below.

Once the payment is made, our credit services team will reconnect your service as soon as possible and no later than the next business day.

Contact us

Between 8:00am to 5:30pm Monday to Friday call the Credit Services Team on 0800 477 333 and pressing Option 1, Option 4, Option 1

Between 5:30pm to 10:00pm Monday to Friday & 8.00am to 10.00pm Saturday to Sunday, call the Customer Services Team on 0800 477 333
